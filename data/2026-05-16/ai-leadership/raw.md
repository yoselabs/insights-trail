- 95% of AI transformation failures trace back to organizational factors: workforce unpreparedness, missing governance, absence of executive ownership, incorrect sequencing
- Four critical success factors: workflow mapping before technology selection, governance embedded in system design from day one, observability established before production launch, leadership continuity through first 18 months

- Iterative pattern: start small, learn, expand
- Agentic implementations showed 71% median productivity gains vs 40% for high-automation systems
- Model choice was fully interchangeable for 42% of implementations — durable advantage is in orchestration, data, and process
