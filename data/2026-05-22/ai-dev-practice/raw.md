## DEEP FETCH: MANUS CONTEXT ENGINEERING

Source: https://manus.im/blog/Context-Engineering-for-AI-Agents-Lessons-from-Building-Manus

**Six primary techniques:**
1. KV-Cache Optimization: 10x cost difference cached vs uncached (Claude Sonnet $0.30 vs $3.00 per million tokens); maintain stable prefixes; append-only modifications
2. Masking not removal: avoid dynamically adding/removing tools mid-iteration (invalidates KV-cache, confuses model)
3. File system as extended memory: unlimited, persistent, restorable; store URLs/paths not full content
4. Attention manipulation via recitation: maintain living todo lists in recent context to prevent "lost-in-the-middle" degradation
5. Preserve error evidence: keep failed actions visible; allows models to "implicitly update internal beliefs"
6. Break few-shot patterns: introduce controlled randomness to prevent blind pattern repetition
