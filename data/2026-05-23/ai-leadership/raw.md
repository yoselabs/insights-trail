### CTO Strategy & Enterprise Adoption

**Amazing CTO: AI Roadmap for CTOs**
- **URL:** https://www.amazingcto.com/ai-roadmap-for-ctos/
- **9-Level Framework:**
  - Level 1: AI as research tool (replacing Stack Overflow)
  - Level 2: Read-only code analysis
  - Level 3: Daily AI usage across team (critical milestone)
  - Level 4-6: AI writes code (bug fixes, functions, prototypes)
  - Level 7: AI generates, humans review everything
  - Level 8: AI deploys with guardrails, not human review
  - Level 9: Software disappears; outcomes replace code
- **Key quote:** "Match risk to capability—your CRUD endpoints can be at level 7 while payment processing stays at level 3."
- **Leadership shift:** At Level 8, role becomes defining guardrails and accepting responsibility.
